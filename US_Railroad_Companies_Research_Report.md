## 1. UNION PACIFIC CORPORATION (NYSE: UNP)

### COMPANY OVERVIEW & HISTORY

**Headquarters:** Omaha, Nebraska  
**Founded:** 1862  
**CEO:** Jim Vena  
**Market Cap:** ~$158 billion  
**Employees:** ~32,000

Union Pacific is one of America's leading transportation companies, with operations spanning 23 western states. The company traces its roots to the Pacific Railroad Act of 1862, signed by President Abraham Lincoln, which authorized the construction of the first transcontinental railroad. UP operates approximately 32,452 route miles of track and is the largest railroad network in the western United States.

### BUSINESS MODEL & OPERATIONS

**Geographic Footprint:**
- Operates in 23 western U.S. states
- Connections to Mexico and Canada
- Access to all major West Coast and Gulf Coast ports
- Critical link in transcontinental freight movement

**Business Segments:**
1. **Bulk** - Coal, renewable energy products, petroleum products, chemicals, plastics
2. **Industrial** - Construction materials, minerals, metals, food products
3. **Premium** - Automotive, intermodal containers, finished goods
4. **Agricultural** - Grain, grain products, fertilizers, food and beverage

**Operating Strategy:**
- Precision Scheduled Railroading (PSR) principles
- Focus on safety, service, and operational excellence
- Network optimization and asset efficiency
- Technology-driven operations

### MANAGEMENT QUALITY & LEADERSHIP

**CEO Jim Vena** took leadership with a clear mandate to drive operational improvements. His background includes:
- Former COO of Canadian National Railway
- Deep expertise in Precision Scheduled Railroading
- Focus on safety culture and operational metrics
- Industry veteran with proven track record

Management has demonstrated commitment to:
- Industry-leading financial performance
- Capital discipline and shareholder returns
- Workforce productivity improvements
- Safety as top priority

### COMPETITIVE POSITIONING & MARKET SHARE

**Competitive Advantages:**

**Market Position:**

### STRATEGIC INITIATIVES & GROWTH PLANS
