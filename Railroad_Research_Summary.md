## KEY FINDINGS BY COMPANY

### 1. UNION PACIFIC (UNP) - INDUSTRY LEADER
**Strengths:**
- Best-in-class operating ratio: 59.9% (industry leading)
- Largest western US rail network
- 2024 EPS: $11.09 (+6% YoY)
- Revenue: $24.3B
- Dividend yield: ~2.4%
- ROIC: 15.8%

**Investment Profile:** Premium quality, defensive growth, best operator

---

### 2. CSX CORPORATION (CSX) - EASTERN LEADER
**Strengths:**
- Leading eastern US railroad
- 2024 Revenue: $14.5B
- Improving operating metrics
- Strong intermodal franchise
- Port access advantage

**Investment Profile:** Quality eastern exposure, steady performer

---

### 3. NORFOLK SOUTHERN (NSC) - TURNAROUND STORY
**Strengths:**
- Dramatic operational improvement (1,010 bps OR improvement in 2024!)
- 2024 EPS: $11.57 (+44% YoY)
- Operating ratio improved from 76.5% to 66.4%
- Volume growth: +5% (2024)
- Strong momentum continuing

**Investment Profile:** Operational turnaround, margin expansion potential

---

### 4. CANADIAN PACIFIC KANSAS CITY (CP) - GROWTH STORY
**Strengths:**
- ONLY railroad connecting Canada-US-Mexico
- Direct exposure to nearshoring/Mexico manufacturing boom
- 2024 Revenue: $14.5B (+5% YoY)
- Merger synergies: $1B+ expected
- Best positioned for USMCA growth

**Investment Profile:** High growth potential, nearshoring beneficiary

---

### 5. CANADIAN NATIONAL (CNI) - DIVIDEND GROWTH
**Strengths:**
- Canada's largest railroad
- 2024 Revenue: CAD $17B / USD $12.4B
- Dividend increased 5% in Q4 2024
- Transcontinental network
- Diverse commodity base
- Strong dividend history

**Investment Profile:** Income-oriented, Canadian exposure

---

### 6. WABTEC (WAB) - EQUIPMENT LEADER
**Strengths:**
- Market leader in railroad equipment
- Revenue: ~$11.2B
- Market cap: $45B
- 50% recurring revenue (aftermarket/services)
- Technology advantage (digital solutions)
- Locomotive modernization tailwind

**Investment Profile:** Technology + services, recurring revenue model

## INDUSTRY TRENDS & OUTLOOK

### MAJOR POSITIVE TRENDS:
✓ **Nearshoring/Reshoring** - Manufacturing returning to North America (huge for CP)
✓ **Sustainability** - Rail 3-4x more fuel efficient than trucks
✓ **Technology adoption** - Digital solutions improving efficiency
✓ **Pricing power** - Inflation protection
✓ **PSR implementation** - Industry-wide efficiency gains

### CHALLENGES:
⚠ Economic uncertainty and recession risks
⚠ Labor cost inflation
⚠ Coal volume secular decline
⚠ Regulatory scrutiny (post-East Palestine derailment)

### 2025-2026 OUTLOOK:
- Low-to-mid single digit revenue growth
- Continued operating ratio improvements
- Margin expansion from productivity
- Stable-to-growing dividends
- Mid-to-high single digit EPS growth

## BOTTOM LINE

The US railroad industry is highly consolidated with 5 dominant Class I freight railroads offering:
- Natural monopoly characteristics (high barriers to entry)
- Pricing power and inflation protection
- Improving efficiency through PSR
- Sustainability tailwinds
- Steady cash flows and dividend growth
- Essential infrastructure with defensive characteristics

**Best Overall Investment:** Union Pacific (UNP) - highest quality, best operator
**Best Growth Story:** Canadian Pacific Kansas City (CP) - Mexico/nearshoring exposure
**Best Turnaround:** Norfolk Southern (NSC) - dramatic operational improvement
**Best Dividend Growth:** Canadian National (CNI) - consistent dividend increases
**Best Equipment Play:** Wabtec (WAB) - market leader with technology edge

All Class I railroads offer defensive growth with steady dividends - choice depends on:
- Risk tolerance
- Growth vs income preference
- Geographic/thematic exposure desired
- Valuation entry point
