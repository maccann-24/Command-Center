## EXECUTION PLAYBOOK

### Phase 1: IDENTIFY
**Scan for:**
- Sanctioned countries/companies
- Assets trading at 70%+ discounts to historical value
- Binary political outcomes (regime change, sanctions lift, peace deals)
- Forced sellers creating liquidity crisis

### Phase 2: RESEARCH
**For each opportunity:**
- What's the catalyst? (Specific event, not vague "things get better")
- What's the timeline? (6 months? 5 years?)
- What's the base case value? (What should it trade at if normalized?)
- What's the current price? (Discount calculation)
- How do you access it? (US exchange, OTC, international broker, bonds?)

### Phase 3: POSITION
**Sizing rules:**
- **Direct frozen assets:** 1-3% max (binary outcome, high risk)
- **Indirect plays:** 3-5% (moderate risk)
- **Derivative plays:** 5-10% (lower risk)
- **Total geopolitical book:** 15-20% max of portfolio

**Entry strategy:**
- Start small (0.5-1% position)
- Add on confirmation (peace talks announced, sanctions talk begins)
- Max out if deal imminent (but before it's done)

### Phase 4: MONITOR
**Weekly checks:**
- News from target countries
- Sanctions website updates (OFAC, EU)
- Political developments (elections, coups, summits)

**Triggers to ADD:**
- Positive diplomatic signals
- Sanctions waivers/exemptions begin
- International pressure for normalization

**Triggers to EXIT:**
- Deal falls apart
- Regime hardens stance
- Sanctions escalate
- Time decay (thesis taking too long)

### Phase 5: EXIT
**Selling discipline:**
- Sell 50% on deal announcement (lock in gains)
- Sell 25% on implementation begins
- Hold 25% for full normalization (capture last leg up)

**Don't be greedy:** 
- 500% gain is life-changing
- Don't need to catch 2000%
- Take profits systematically

## RISK MANAGEMENT

### Political Risks:
- **Regime change fails** - Opposition loses, sanctions harden
- **Deal collapses** - Negotiations break down
- **Black swan** - War, coup, assassination

### Market Risks:
- **Liquidity** - Can't exit frozen positions
- **Currency** - Devaluation wipes out gains
- **Nationalization** - Government seizes assets

### Legal Risks:
- **Sanctions violations** - Buying/holding prohibited assets
- **Tax complexity** - Foreign tax credits, PFIC reporting
- **Fraud** - Scams targeting distressed asset buyers

### Mitigations:
- ✅ Size positions appropriately (never more than 3% in one frozen asset)
- ✅ Use indirect plays when direct access blocked
- ✅ Consult OFAC sanctions list before buying
- ✅ Work with international tax specialist
- ✅ Accept that 50-70% of these trades will fail
